╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                    QUICK START STEPS                                  ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Step 1: Login
  → Use your Admin, Manager, or Accountant credentials

Step 2: Find Expenses
  → Look at the left sidebar
  → Find the section mentioned above for your role
  → Click on "📊 Expenses"

Step 3: Create Your First Expense
  → Click the green "+ Create Expense" button
  → Fill in the form:
     - Title (required)
     - Category (required)
     - Amount (required)
     - Date (required)
  → Click "Create Expense"

Step 4: Manage Expenses
  → Use filters to find expenses
  → Click action buttons to manage
  → View statistics at the top


╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                    TROUBLESHOOTING                                    ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Problem: Can't see "Expenses" in sidebar
Solution: 
  1. Refresh page (Ctrl + F5)
  2. Check your role (must be Admin, Manager, or Accountant)
  3. Logout and login again

Problem: Clicking does nothing
Solution:
  1. Check browser console (F12)
  2. Ensure server is running
  3. Try direct URL access

Problem: Get 404 error
Solution:
  1. Verify files exist in client/src/pages/
  2. Check App.js has routes configured
  3. Restart development server
